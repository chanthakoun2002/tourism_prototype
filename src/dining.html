Dining And Cuisine Taniti

Local Flavours

Taniti’s cuisine is a blend of fresh local ingredients and cultural influences. There are five restaurants specializing in fresh fish, rice, and island-style dishes, offering a taste of authentic Tanitian cuisine.

American Favorites

If you're craving something familiar, Taniti has three restaurants offering American-style meals like burgers, sandwiches, and steaks for american travelers who want comfort food away from home.

Pan-Asian Cuisine

Two Pan-Asian restaurants offer a wide variety of dishes inspired by Chinese, Japanese, and Thai cooking. Whether you're in the mood for noodles or sushi, there’s something for everyone.

Grocery Options

Prefer to cook your own meals or need snacks for your adventure? Taniti offers two full-sized supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and a 24-hour convenience store for your everyday needs.
