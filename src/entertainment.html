Entertainment And activities On Tanini

Beaches & Nature

Most visitors come to Taniti to relax on its stunning beaches or explore its lush rainforests. The island’s diverse terrain includes white sandy beaches, rocky shorelines, and an active volcano.

Outdoor Adventures

Snorkeling, zip-lining through the rainforest, helicopter rides, and guided volcano tours await thrill-seekers. You can also go on hikes, take boat tours, or you can join chartered fishing trips across the island.

Nightlife & Family Fun

Enjoy the island’s pubs, microbrewery, and dance club after dark. Family activities include an arcade, bowling alley, and a movie theater. A nine-hole golf course is set to open soon.

Merriton Landing

Many of the island's various activities are centered in Merriton Landing, a growing tourist hub on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay. It is the perfect place to stay, dine, and explore.

Sightseeing and other activities

Most tourists spend most of their time in taniti City, which boasts native architecture and nearby white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay. Other activities that can be enjoyed are bus or boat tours of the island, hikes into tanitis rainforest, or a visit to tanitis active volcano.
