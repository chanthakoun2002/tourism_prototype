Lodgings On Tanini

From Budget to Luxury

Taniti offers a wide range of lodging options to meet any budget. For budget consious visitors they can find affordable stays at a local hostel and budget hotels, while those seeking comfort and amenities can enjoy the island’s large, four-star resort with pools, spa services, and ocean views.

Local Family-Run Hotels

A favorite for many travelers, Taniti’s small family-owned hotels provide a warm, personalized experience with hosts who know the island well. These accommodations are spread throughout the island and offer an unique character and charm for any visitors.

Bed and Breakfast

For those looking for a cozy stay and homemade meals, the growing number of bed and breakfasts across Taniti make for a relaxing and enjoyable, immersive experience. Many are located in quant scenic areas, perfect for nature lovers.

Regulated and Safe

All lodgings on taniti both small and large on the island are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government to ensure cleanliness, safety, and quality standards for visitors.
